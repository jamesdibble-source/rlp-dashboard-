## WHAT WE MUST BUILD (prioritised)

### CRITICAL — Without these we're not credible
1. **Median calculations** — median price, median lot size, median $/m² at every level (metro, corridor, LGA, suburb). Average is not good enough. Every competitor uses median.
2. **QoQ and YoY deltas** — every metric needs % change vs prior quarter and prior year
3. **Quarterly sales volume** — total sold lots per quarter per corridor/metro
4. **Full corridor coverage** — South Eastern (Casey/Cardinia), Geelong, plus the ~48 R4 submarkets as our corridor definitions
5. **National data** — at minimum Melbourne, SEQ, Adelaide, Perth, Sydney with comparable metrics so we can show cross-market comparison
6. **Price trend charts** — median price and $/m² over at least 4 quarters, ideally 2+ years
7. **Months of trading stock** — current listings ÷ monthly sales rate. This is the single best supply indicator.

### HIGH — What makes us competitive
8. **Suburb price map at standard lot sizes** — OH's killer feature. Normalised price at 400m² and 448m² (VIC), 375m² and 400m² (SEQ), 300m² and 375m² (SA) per suburb.
9. **Sales volume trend charts** — quarterly sales going back as far as we have data
10. **Lot size trends** — median lot size QoQ/YoY with trend chart
11. **DOM tracking** — average days on market per corridor. RPM has this, most others don't.
12. **Lot size distribution** — 50m² bands (300-350, 350-400, etc.) showing count and % of sales
13. **Cross-market comparison page** — all capital cities side by side (price, size, $/m², sales, stock)

### DIFFERENTIATORS — What no competitor does
14. **Daily updates** — RPM/OH/R4 are quarterly, GRIP is monthly. We update daily.
15. **Individual lot drill-down** — click suburb → see every lot with address, price, size, date
16. **835+ suburbs discovered** vs OH's 27 mapped suburbs and RPM's ~50 tracked
17. **Sold Only toggle** — strip current listings, show only transacted prices
18. **Free access** — R4 is expensive subscription, OH/RPM are gated. We're open.
19. **Search + sort** — find any suburb instantly, sort by any column
20. **Custom corridor definitions** — user picks their own suburbs to compare
